Welcome to CMCS College, Nashik

Commerce, Management & Computer Science (CMCS) College was started by Maratha Vidya Prasarak (MVP) Samaj in September 2006. Since June 2009 the College has started functioning on its own campus.

Inaugration of the new majestic and well planned college building with ground plus five floors was done on Saturday 7th April 2012 at the hands of Hon. Sharad Pawar, Minister of Agriculture, Government of India.

All the 14 classrooms, library, study room, laboratories and the administrative section are well furnished and fully equipped.
